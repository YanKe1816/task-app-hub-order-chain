Shipping Delay Extractor

Shipping Delay Extractor extracts shipping delay notice fields from user-provided notice text. It is read-only and deterministic.

The app does not store data, create accounts, use authentication, use external APIs, contact carriers, send emails, modify orders, or write to external systems.

The app does not provide compensation decisions or responsibility decisions. It only returns structured fields and warnings from the provided text.
