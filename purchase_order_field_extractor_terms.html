Terms

Purchase Order Field Extractor is a deterministic field extraction utility for review workflows.

Use limitation

The app extracts fields from provided text. It is not a purchase approval system, payment system, supplier communication system, inventory system, tax calculator, or order submission system.

No procurement advice

The app does not provide professional procurement, legal, financial, accounting, or business approval advice.

Read-only operation

The app does not create, approve, submit, modify, send, validate, or pay purchase orders and does not contact suppliers or update external systems.
