Invoice Field Extractor is a deterministic field extraction utility for review workflows.

Use limitation

The app extracts fields from provided invoice text. It is not an invoice approval system, payment system, supplier communication system, tax advisory system, accounting advisory system, bookkeeping system, or order modification system.

No professional advice

The app does not provide tax, accounting, legal, financial, procurement, or business approval advice.

Read-only operation

The app does not approve, reject, pay, modify, send, or submit invoices and does not contact suppliers or update external systems.
