Invoice Field Extractor processes only the text provided to the tool for field extraction.

No login

The app does not require user accounts, authentication, or login credentials.

No storage

The app is stateless and does not store invoice text, extracted fields, or user data.

No external APIs

The app does not call external APIs and does not send provided text to third-party services.

No external writes

The app does not write to finance systems, accounting systems, supplier systems, email systems, databases, or any other external system.
